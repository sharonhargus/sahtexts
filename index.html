This website contains recordings and accompanying texts in the Ichishkiin (Yakama Sahaptin) language, as well as translations in English. For best viewing of these texts, we recommend opening this website in a desktop or laptop internet browser. Mozilla Firefox seems to be the best choice for displaying special characters in the texts. You can download Firefox here. Funding for this project was in part provided by The Jacobs Research Funds, Native Voices Endowment (The Endangered Languages Fund), and the University of Washington (Royalty Research Fund and the Department of Linguistics).
